APPENDIX B - Constraints
Animation Related Organizing Principle

Definition

For every <Animation Related Geometry> instance A with N <Geometry Hierarchy> components, the following shall hold:

  1. No <Geometry Hierarchy> instance shall appear more than once as a component of A.

  2. For every <Animation Behaviour> component B of A,

    1. The beginning_frame field of B shall have a value in the range 1..N.

    2. The ending_frame field of B shall have a value in the range beginning_frame..N.
