APPENDIX B - Constraints
Polyhedron Structure

Definition

The collection of <Polygon> components of a <Polyhedron> instance shall completely enclose a region of 3D space.

Rationale

The geometric structure of a <Polyhedron> instance shall be complete and consistent.

Example

  1. A <Polyhedron> instance representing a cube has six <Polygon> components, one for each face of the cube.
